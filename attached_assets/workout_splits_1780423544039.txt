Workout Splits:

Full Body
1 compound movement (chest, back, legs)
3 circuits (2 exercises each), 1 chest, 1 back, 1, legs, 1 core, 1 arm (bicep or tricep), 1 shoulder

Upper/Lower Body
	Upper body
		1 compound movement (chest, back)
		3 circuits (2 exercises each)
			Circuit 1: 1 chest, 1 back + arms
			Circuit 2: 1 chest, 1 arm
			Circuit 3: 1 back, 1 arm

	Lower body
		1 compound movement (legs)
		3 circuits (2 exercises each)
			Circuit 1: 2 legs
			Circuit 2: 2 legs
			Circuit 3: 2 legs

Upper Body/Lower Body + Core
	Upper body
		1 compound movement (chest, back)
		3 circuits (2 exercises each)
			Circuit 1: 1 chest, 1 back + arms
			Circuit 2: 1 chest, 1 arm
			Circuit 3: 1 back, 1 arm

	Lower body
		1 compound movement (legs)
		3 circuits (2 exercises each)
			Circuit 1: 1 leg, 1 core
			Circuit 2: 1 leg, 1 core
			Circuit 3: 1 leg, 1 core

Push/Pull/Legs
	Push 
		1 compound movement (chest or shoulders)
		3 circuits (2 exercises each)
			Circuit 1: 1 chest, 1 arm (tricep)
			Circuit 2: 1 chest, 1 shoulder
			Circuit 3: 1 shoulder, 1 arm (tricep)

	Pull
		1 compound movement (back)
		3 circuits (2 exercises each)
			Circuit 1: 1 back, 1 arm (biceps)
			Circuit 2: 1 back, 1 lats
			Circuit 3: 1 lats, 1 arm (biceps)

	Legs
		1 compound movement (legs)
		3 circuits (2 exercises each)
			Circuit 1: 2 legs
			Circuit 2: 2 legs
			Circuit 3: 2 legs

Push/Pull/Legs + Core
	Push 
		1 compound movement (chest or shoulders)
		3 circuits (2 exercises each)
			Circuit 1: 1 chest, 1 arm (tricep)
			Circuit 2: 1 chest, 1 shoulder
			Circuit 3: 1 shoulder, 1 arm (tricep)

	Pull
		1 compound movement (back)
		3 circuits (2 exercises each)
			Circuit 1: 1 back, 1 arm (biceps)
			Circuit 2: 1 back, 1 lats
			Circuit 3: 1 lats, 1 arm (biceps)

	Legs
		1 compound movement (legs)
		1 compound movement (legs)
		3 circuits (2 exercises each)
			Circuit 1: 1 leg, 1 core
			Circuit 2: 1 leg, 1 core
			Circuit 3: 1 leg, 1 core
